SEE Exam Rules

  • Arrive 30 mins before exam start.
  • No electronic devices allowed inside.
  • Carry admit card and valid ID.
  • Follow invigilator instructions strictly.
  • Maintain discipline at all times.
  • No sharing of question papers or answers.
  • Use only allowed stationery.
  • Report any malpractice immediately.
  • Do not disturb other candidates.
  • Follow seating arrangement properly.
  • Bring your own water bottle if needed.
  • Wear proper uniform if required.
  • Stay calm & focused during exams.
